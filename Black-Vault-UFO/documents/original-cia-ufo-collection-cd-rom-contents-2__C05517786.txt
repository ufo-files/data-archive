 PRODUCTION OF A NEW EIGHT-SEATER PASSENGER BUSINESS JET WHOSE

 BRITISH AEROSPACE.   THIS IS THE COMPANY WHICH IS ALSO TAKING PART

 OF COOPERATION AMONG LEADING WESTERN COUNTRIES.
