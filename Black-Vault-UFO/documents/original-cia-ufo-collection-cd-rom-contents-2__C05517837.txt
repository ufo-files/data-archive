 [FBIS Translated Text]      What does the United States really

 which the defense system is aimed --  be it terrorism or a t Treat

 *extraterrestrials?
 Is there really a threat from space?
 In fact, not only NATO Secretary General George Robertson
 implied potential attacks from space, but also US President George
 Bush warned the NATO partners to Brussels of "new threats."
 However, he did not say what kind of threats -- whether a meteorite
 *storm or even UFO attacks.
 Speaking with Biid, Defense Minister Rudolf Scharping (Social
 Democratic Party of Germany) clearly commented on the insinuations
 by NATO Secretary General Robertson about a "threat from space,"
 which could be understood all too easily as panic-mongering against
 *extraterrestrial attacks.
 Scharpings "There is worldwide agreement that the proliferation
 of mass destruction weapons creates new threats.   This requires a
 common and political response -- with clear realism."
