 ((FBIS TRANSLATED TEXT))  /"THERE IS A LOT OF SADNESS IN A LOT OF
 KNOWLEDGE," SAYS ANCIENT WISDOM.  ESPECIALLY AFTER YOU LEARN ABOUT
 SOME NOT VERY PLEASANT FEATURES OF THE PLACE ON EARTH THAT HAS
 BECOME THE ENVIRONMENT YOU INHABIT./
 THUS, UNUSUAL,  IT CAN BE SAID, UNIQUE MAPS WERE COMPILED BY
 SPECIALISTS OF THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND EPIDEMIC CENTER AND A NUMBER OF
 SCIENTIFIC INSTITUTES BY ORDER OF THE MAYORALTY'S COMMITTEE FOR
 ECOLOGY.   THERE IS A NOISE MAP, AND ALSO MAPS OF THE LUMINESCENT AND
 ELECTROMAGNETIC POLLUTION OF NOVOSIBIRSK.  MEDICAL DOCTORS ARE
 CONVINCED THAT THE UNFAVORABLE ECOLOGICAL SITUATION IS CAUSED,  IN
 PARTICULAR, BY THE FACT THAT POWERFUL SOURCES OF NONIONIZING (NOT
 RADIOACTIVE) RADIATION ARE CONCENTRATED IN OUR CITY.   SENSELESS
 URBANIZATION ALSO MADE A CONTRIBUTION, THE MOST TERRIBLE CONSEQUENCE
 OF WHICH IS THE PROGRESSIVE DYING OUT OF THE.POPULATION:  THE
 INCREASE IN THE DEATH RATE IN NOVOSIBIRSK EXCEEDS THE BIRTH RATE;
 AND THE FIGURES ON CANCERS SOUND MORE FRIGHTENING EACH YEAR.  NOISE,
 ELECTROMAGNETIC RADIATION, AND THE UNDERGROUND ENVIRONMENT ARE DOING
 THEIR DIRTY WORK LITTLE BY LITTLE.
 *   MAGNETIC STORMS, UFO'S, EARTHQUAKES... ((SUBHEAD))
 SIX LINES OF SO-CALLED MAIN FAULTS OF. CRYSTALLINE ROCK STRETCH

 CHOSEN FOR ITS FOUNDATION.  NEVERTHELESS, IT IS EXACTLY HERE THAT A
 VERY LARGE INDUSTRIAL CENTER WAS BUILT, AND THE UNDERGROUND GODS
 WERE IRRITATED.
 SOME OF THE FAULTS ARE GROWING CONTINUOUSLY, WHICH CANNOT HELP
 BUT INTEREST GEOLOGISTS:   THE TERRITORY ON WHICH THE CITY STANDS IS

 HAS TO SURFACE FROM TIME TO TIME, AND IT COULD COME OUT IN THE FORM
 OF MAGNETIC STORMS, ELECTROMAGNETIC RADIATION, OR EVEN EARTHQUAKES.
 ALTHOUGH AT PRESENT THERE IS NOT ONE MORE OR LESS SPECIFIC FORECAST
 OF SEISMOLOGISTS CONCERNING THE POSSIBILITY OF UNDERGROUND TREMORS
 IN NOVOSIBIRSK IN THE NEXT FEW YEARS, IT IS IMPOSSIBLE NOT TO TAKE
 SUCH A PROBABILITY INTO ACCOUNT.

 THE SECRET FACTOR((SUBHEAD))
 SCIENTISTS ARE STILL RACKING THEIR BRAINS OVER THE RIDDLE OF
 ELECTROMAGNETIC RADIATION, ALTHOUGH THERE IS NO LONGER ANY DOUBT
 THAT THIS FACTOR IN OUR ENVIRONMENT HAS BY FAR NOT THE BEST EFFECT
 ON A PERSON'S STATE OF HEALTH.   THE ANTENNAS OF RADIOTECHNICAL
 FACILITIES ARE RECOGNIZED SOURCES OF ELECTROMAGNETIC ENERGY.   RADIO
 IS THE MOST DEMOCRATIC (ACCESSIBLE) OF ALL THE SOURCES OF
 INFORMATION AND,  IT TURNS OUT,  IT IS ALSO THE SOURCE OF MANY
 TROUBLES.  THE FIGURE THAT WE WILL NOW CITE SPEAKS FOR ITSELF:
 THERE ARE 158 ENTERPRISES AND INSTITUTIONS IN OUR CITY THAT POSSESS
 RADIOTECHNICAL RESOURCES THAT FORM THE ARTIFICIAL ELECTROMAGNETIC
 BACKGROUND OF THE CITY.   RESEARCHERS'  OBSERVATIONS SHOWED THAT UNDER
 THE INFLUENCE OF AN ELECTROMAGNETIC FIELD, CHANGES OCCUR IN THE
 ENDOCRINE SYSTEM OF THE HUMAN BODY, THERE IS A WEAKENING OF
 IMMUNITY, AND THE FORMULA OF THE BLOOD IS DISRUPTED.  THE LIST COULD
 BE CONTINUED...
