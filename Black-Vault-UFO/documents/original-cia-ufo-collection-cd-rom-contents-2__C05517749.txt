 ((FBIS TRANSCRIBED TEXT))   THE POLICE AUTHORITIES IN TAMALE HAVE
 WARNED RESIDENTS IN AND AROUND THE MUNICIPALITY NOT TO GO NEAR AN
 *UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT WHICH FELL AT (KATURIGU) IN THE
 (?OVERSEAS) AREA OF THE WEST MAMPRUSSI DISTRICT.  THE- WARNING SAID

 FORCE STATIONS IN TAMALE HAVE GONE TO THE AREA TO TRY AND IDENTIFY
 THE OBJECT AFTER A TEAM FROM TEE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AGENCY AND
 SOME SCIENTISTS HAD FAILED TO IDENTIFY IT.   THE OBJECT IS SUSPECTED
 TO BE A MILITARY HARDWARE BECAUSE SOMETHING BELIEVED TO BE A
 PARACHUTE WAS FOUND AMONG THE FRAGMENTS.
