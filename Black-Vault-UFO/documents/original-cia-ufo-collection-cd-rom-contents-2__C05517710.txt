 SUBJ:    MININT MEMBERS CITED ON UFO SIGHTINGS
 SOURCE:  HAVANA RADIO REBELDE NETWORK IN SPANISH 1800 GMT 3 NOV 94
 TEXT:
 //((FROM THE "NATIONAL RADIO NEWS"))
 ((FBIS TRANSLATED TEXT)) THREE UFOS WERE SEEN MIDNIGHT 29 OCTOBER
 IN DIFFERENT PLACES IN LAS TUNAS PROVINCE. THE APPEARANCE OF TWO OF
 THE UFOS IN NIGUA ((2047N, 7646W)), MAJIBACOA MUNICIPALITY, AND THE
 NEARBY PRISON, PUT THE VIGILANCE AND PROTECTION SYSTEM ON IMMEDIATE
 ALERT.
 MEN IN PATROL CARS SENT THERE SAW THE UFOS. TESTIMONIES FROM THE
 CHIEF GUARD OFFICER OF THE MUNICIPAL GENERAL STAFF IN LAS TUNAS, AND
 THE GUARD OFFICER ON DUTY AT THE PRISON, DESCRIBED THE UFOS AS
 BRIGHT AND WITH LIGHTS OF DIFFERENT COLORS. SEVERAL NEIGHBORS AND
 COMBAT MEMBERS OF THE MINISTRY OF THE INTERIOR ((MININT)) LIVING
 CLOSE TO THE MAJIBACOA PRISON BELIEVED THE UFOS TO BE PLANES OR

 OTHER SCIENTISTS STATE THAT UFOS ARE NO MORE THAN AIR CONTAINING A
