 MILITARY AIRFIELD WILL INEVITABLY CO.' UP AGAINST A MUDDLE OF PAPERS

 DEPARTMENTS.

 WAS KNOWN IN BELARUS THAT FOREIGN BALLOONISTS MIGHT BE FLYING OVER
