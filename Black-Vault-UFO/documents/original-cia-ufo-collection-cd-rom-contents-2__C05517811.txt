 TEXT TEXT:
 [REPORT BY CORRESPONDENT]
 UNIDENTIFIEDNFLYINGDOBJECTS    DERA        KONAMONDAYONIGHT IN GAGAN
 THAL, KHAR AND GIRDO-RAKHIGAAJ AREAS OF DERA GHAZI KHAN.   THE
 OBJECTS WERE SEEN PROCEEDING FROM WEST TO EAST.    MONDAY'S SIGHTING
 WAS THE THIRD INCIDENT OF UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS SEEN BY THE
 LOCALS OF DERA AND RAJANPUR.   THE REPORTS ATTAIN IMPORTANCE IN
 VIEW THAT ALL THESE FLYING OBJECTS HAVE BEEN SEEN OVER HIGHLY
 SENSITIVE AREAS OF THE COUNTRY -- THE TRIBAL AREAS OF RAJANPUR AND
 DERA GHAZI KHAN.   MEANWHILE, THE MYSTERY OF AN UNIDENTIFIED FLYING
 OBJECT SEEN IN THE TRIBAL AREA OF DERA GHAZI KHAN ON SUNDAY NIGHT
 REMAINED UNRESOLVED.   AUTHORITIES ADMITTED THAT A LIGHT IN THE SKY
 WAS SEEN IN THE AREA BUT INTELLIGENCE AGENCIES AND BORDER MILITARY
 POLICE HAD FAILED TO LOCATE ANY FLYING OBJECT AT ROUGHIN,
 BUGHALCHUR (URANIUM FIELD) AND CEMENT FACTORY AREA.
