 (FBIS TRANSCRIBED TEXT]      MOSCOW, NOV 16 (INTERFAX) --
 RUSSIA'S AIR FORCE DISMISSED ON THURSDAY REPORTS THAT AN
 UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT WAS SIGHTED IN RUSSIA EARLY ON TUESDAY.

 REGION SAID GUARDS AT TWO RUSSIAN FRONTIER POSTS SAW A UFO AT 1.45
 A.M. NEAR RUSSIA'S BORDER WITH AZERBAIJAN.

 FILYA IN DAGESTAN'S MAGARAMKENT DISTRICT, SAID THEY WATCHED AN
 OBJECT FLYING QUICKLY AT AN ALTITUDE OF ABOUT 100 METERS FOR TWO
 MINUTES.
