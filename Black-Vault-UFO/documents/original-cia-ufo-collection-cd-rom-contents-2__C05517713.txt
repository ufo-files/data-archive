 SENKO HAS OVERSTEPPED THE BOUNDS OF "LIBERAL DEMOCRACY." WHAT
 LEADER COULD BE PLEASED THAT SOMEONE WAS COMPETING WITH HIM, EVEN AT
 THE LOCAL LEVEL? AND VYACHESLAV IGORYEVICH HAD EVERYWHERE ASSERTED
 THAT HE WAS A ZHIRINOVSKIY ON THE URALS SCALE, AND THEN HE DECLARED
 THAT HE WAS A SPIRITUAL PRECEPTOR OF THE LDPR. THERE WAS MORE
 FURTHER ON. QUOTATIONS FROM THE "BRILLIANT" THOUGHTS OF THE LEADER
 GAVE WAY TO POSTULATES FROM THE BIBLE; AN ICON OCCUPIED THE SACRED
 CORNER AT HEADQUARTERS, AND TOGETHER WITH THE OBLAST ORGANIZATION'S
 "SPIRITUAL ADVISER," A. MINAKOV, THE COORDINATOR SENT THE CHAIRMAN
 OF THE PARTY A PROPOSAL ON THE NECESSITY OF CHANGING THE RULES IN
 FAVOR OF RELIGION.
 HAVING "LULLED GOD TO SLEEP," SENKO HOOKED UP WITH "UNCLEAN
 FORCES." SUDDENLY HIS DEPUTY BECAME A CERTAIN S. KUKURIN, ADVERTISED
 AS "THE DIRECTOR OF THE TIME LABORATORY." VYACHESLAV IGORYEVICH
 HASTENED TO SUPPORT HIS IDEA TO CREATE UNDER THE LDPR A "RUSSIAN
 SCIENTIFIC-RESEARCH LABORATORY OF ANOMALOUS PHENOMENA AND
 'EXTRATERRESTRIAL CIVILIZATIONS," WITH WHICH HE WISHED TO MAKE HIS
 LEADER HAPPY. DURING ONE OF HIS VISITS TO MOSCOW, HE, TOGETHER WITH
 ?KUKURIN, HANDED V.V.ZH. A TEST TUBE WITH AN "EXTRATERRESTRIAL
 SUBSTANCE"; ZHIRINOVSKIY,  IN SENKO'S WORDS, THREW IT IN HIS DESK.
 BUT SINCE THE CHAIRMAN DID NOT OBJECT TO THE INNOVATION, THE
 "LABORATORY" BEGAN TO UNFURL ITS ACTIVITY. IN THE BEGINNING OF
 JANUARY OF THIS YEAR,  IT PUBLISHED A STRANNAYA GAZETA (STRANGE
 NEWSPAPER), WITH A "GLOBAL SENSATION" IN THE SPIRIT OF THE MOODS OF
 THE LEADER OF THE PARTY. UNDER THE HEADLINE,  "1995--A YEAR OF
 REFLECTIONS" WAS A MAP OF THE WORLD... WITHOUT NORTH AMERICA, AND
 BESIDE IT, IN BIG LETTERS, THE DICTUM OF TIME LABORATORY DIRECTOR S.
 KUKURIN:  "WE ARE GIVING THE AMERICANS A CHANCE TO SURVIVE. OVER THE
 COURSE OF ONE YEAR, THEY MUST CHANGE THEIR POLICY AND STOP RUINING
 RUSSIA.  IF THIS DOES NOT OCCUR, THEN THEIR CONTINGENT WILL BE
 COMPLETELY DESTROYED!" THERE'S A GOOD-BYE ((PREVIOUS WORD IN
 ENGLISH, TRANSLITERATED)) FOR YOU. PRESIDENT CLINTON SHOULD NOT ONLY
 PROHIBIT THE ENTRY INTO THE COUNTRY OF THE CHAIRMAN OF THE LDPR
 STATE DUMA FACTION, BUT SHOULD ALSO TAKE PREVENTATIVE MEASURES.
 IT WOULD BE FUNNY TO READ AND LISTEN TO THE INVENTOR OF THE "NEW
 THEORY OF TIME," TO WHOM "THE MECHANISMS OF THE LAUNCHING OF GLOBAL
 CATASTROPHES ARE KNOWN," BUT AFTER ALL,  IT WAS SERIOUSLY SUPPORTED
 BY THE DOCTOR OF PHILOSO?HICAL SCIENCES L. KLENOV, ANOTHER FORMER
 DEPUTY OF SENKO'S. MOREOVER,  THE HEAD OF THE PARTY'S CENTRAL App,;OV

 SENKO IS NO BOLIVAR((SUBHEAD))
 THE TIME HAS COME TO ASK WHY I TURN' TO THE IMAGE OF BOLIVAR--THE
 HORSE FROM THE 0. HENRY NOVELLA "THE ROADS THAT WE CHOOSE." HE WAS
 PROMPTED BY SENKO HIMSELF. AT HIS LAST MEETING WITH JOURNALISTS
 BEFORE THE CONFERENCE,  HE ANNOUNCED AS MUCH: "I AM NO BOLIVAR, TO
 CARRY EVERYONE." FROM FURTHER EXPLICATION,  IT TRANSPIRED THAT HE
 COULD IN FACT CARRY OUT THIS ROLE FURTHER, BUT NOT FOR FREE. IN HIS
 WORDS, MANY LDPR COORDINATORS RECEIVE THE SALARY OF AIDES TO STATE
 DUMA DEPUTIES FROM ZHIRINOVSKIY'S FACTION, AND ONE OF THESE--
 ZVANOV---WAS COMMISSIONED BY VLADIMIR VOLFOVICH TO TAKE SENKO. IVANOV
 ADDRESSED A PAPER TO THE NAME OF THE HEAD OF ADMINISTRATION OF
 SVERDLOVSK OBLAST, WITH THE REQUEST TO DRAW UP THE APPROPRIATE
 DOCUMENT, AND THE URALS COORDINATOR HIMSELF WAS PRESENTED WITH SOLID
 CERTIFICATION AS A DEPUTY'S AIDE. WHEN THE INSPIRED SENXO APPEARED
 AT THE OBLAST ADMINISTRATION,  THERE HE WAS SHOWN ANOTHER LETTER BY
 THE DEPUTY IVANOV: NOT TO CONCLUDE THE AGREEMENT WITH THE PREVIOUSLY
 RECOMT'ENDED CANDIDACY.
 "IF THIS QUESTION IS NOT RESOLVED BEFORE THE CONFERENCE,  I WILL
 SUSPEND MY MEMBERSHIP IN THE PARTY," VYACHESLAV IGORYEVICH TOLD US.
 HOWEVER, AS IT SEEMS TO ME,  THE GIVEN QUESTION WAS NOT THE MAIN
 ONE IN THE FATE OF THE COORDINATOR, FOR HE SPOKE MORE ABOUT
 SOMETHING ELSE.   ABOUT A CONSPIRACY WITH THE AIM OF SEIZING POWER IN
 THE OBLAST PARTY ORGANIZATION: "THERE HAVE BEEN ATTEMPTS AT THE
 REMOVAL BY ANY MEANS (UP TO AND INCLUDING THE PHYSICAL!) OF TRUE
 MEMBERS OF THE LDPR. A. VANISHED FROM BOGDANOVICH; B. FLED KAMENSK-
 URALSKIY FROM TERRORISTS; AND K. DOES NOT MAKE ANY CONTACT FROM
 ARTEMOVSKIY BECAUSE OF PRESSURE FROM KNOWN PERSONS. THERE HAVE
 THREADS OF PHYSICAL REPRISALS WITH RESPECT TO SENKO."
 WHO CAN IT BE WHO IS TERRORIZING THE URALS ZHIRINOVSKIYITES? THE
 COORDINATOR ANSWERED: "SOMEONE." ???
 TO TELL THE TRUTH, MY SUSPICION FELL AT THAT TIME UPON S. KUKURIN
 AND L. KLENOV.  IT WAS THEY WHO,  IN THE ABSENCE OF SENKO, PUT
 TOGETHER THE PRESS CONFERENCE, WHERE THEY SPOKE MURKILY ABOUT THEIR
 LEADER, ABOUT THE CONDITION OF HIS HEALTH, FROM WHICH IT BECAME
 CLEAR: POWER HAD CHANGED HANDS.
