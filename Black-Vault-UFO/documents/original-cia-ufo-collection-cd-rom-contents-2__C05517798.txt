 . HOWEVER,     THE BULLETIN WILL BE CARRYING EXCLUSIVE ARTICLES WHICH
 ARE LIKELY TO ATTRACT A HEALTHY READERSHIP.   THE FIRST ISSUE WILL
 FEATURE AN ARTICLE ENTITLED "WHAT THE COSMONAUTS SAW",  WITH A
 TRANSCRIPT OF THE CONVERSATION BETWEEN TWO SOVIET COSMONAUTS ABOARD
